For a Good Night's Sleep

Tips
6 hours before bed- Try to eliminate consumption of caffeine (after 2 PM)

3 hours before bed- Stop drinking alcohol | If you choose to drink alcohol limit intake to 1-2 drinks per hour and 4 or fewer drinks on any one occasion. Any more than this may interfere with your sleep cycle.

2 hours before bed- Stop exercising | Try to do this 5-6 hours before you go to bed to allow your body to unwind from the workout

0.5 hours before bed- Shut off electronics at least 30 minutes before bed. The blue light disrupts melatonin production, an essential sleep regulating hormone.

Night Time Snacks

Try to snack at least 2 hours before bedtime. This allows time for digestion, so you will sleep more peacefully and avoid instances of reflux for those who have it.

A cheese stick and a few crackers

Whole grain toast with nut butter

One serving of nuts and fruit

Yogurt

One glass of low fat milk

Cottage cheese with fruit

Hummus with raw veggies

Hardboiled egg & a few crackers

Cheese and a few pretzels

Fruit with nut butter

Lean lunch meat with cheese and a slice of whole grain bread

Oatmeal with milk

Note: These suggestions are night time snack ideas that provide an optimal balance of nutrients, but are not calorically regulated. You may need to eat more or less depending on your body size, weight goals, etc.
