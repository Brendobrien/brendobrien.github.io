Pre-Workout Fuel

What to eat an hour before your workout
Fuel by eating a handful of complex carbohydrates along with some protein and 20 oz of fluids up to 30 minutes before your workout. This will provide a lasting, slow-burn energy source.

Examples

Dry cereal and nuts

Plain greek yogurt with fruit or granola

Cereal with yogurt (8 oz)

1/2 of a peanut butter & jelly sandwich

Plain greek yogurt (8 oz) & unsweetened apple sauce

Unsweetened apple sauce and nuts

String cheese & fruit or pretzels

Banana with a tablespoon of peanut butter

Nuts and fresh or dried fruit

Graham crackers & almond butter

Note: These suggestions are snack ideas that provide an optimal balance of nutrients, but are not calorically regulated. You may need to eat more or less depending on your body size, weight goals, etc.
