For a Good Night's Sleep

Tips
6 hours before bed- Try to eliminate consumption of caffeine (after 2 PM)

3 hours before bed- Stop drinking alcohol | If you choose to drink alcohol limit intake to 1-2 drinks per hour and 4 or fewer drinks on any one occasion. Any more than this may interfere with your sleep cycle.

2 hours before bed- Stop exercising | Try to do this 5-6 hours before you go to bed to allow your body to unwind from the workout

0.5 hours before bed- Shut off electronics at least 30 minutes before bed. The blue light disrupts melatonin production, an essential sleep regulating hormone.

Night Time Snacks

Try to snack at least 2 hours before bed. Allowing time for digestion is important to avoid any reflux.
